Frequently Asked Questions

What fund types does Cosimo support?

Private equity, venture, credit, and real estate funds. Cosimo adapts to your fund's reporting conventions, waterfall structures, and document formats.

How does Cosimo ensure audit readiness?

Every figure in every deliverable traces back to its source document. Cosimo doesn't summarize — it cites. Reviewers can verify any number in the output against the original.

What happens to my data?

Every client runs on a fully segregated instance. Your documents, fund structures, and LPA terms never leave your environment and never train a shared model.

How is Cosimo different from general-purpose AI tools?

Generic AI produces text. Cosimo produces deliverables — formatted K-1 packages, structured LP reports, rent roll extractions — built on your fund's actual data and conventions.

What does the pilot cost?

Nothing upfront. We scope a pilot around your highest-pain use case and run it for two full quarters on real deadlines. If it doesn't change how your fund operates, walk away.
